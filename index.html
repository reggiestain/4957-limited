About Us

Forty Nine 57

Globalization is contributing to rapid changes in Africa's economy andas such economic integration and transformation is paramount to oursuccess. Forty Nine 57 recognizes this evolution and we havepositioned ourselves as part of the drivers of economic integration inGhana.
Forty Nine 57 Limited uses a social enterprise approach as a catalystfor increasing access, choice, and quality for medical products andequipment, education tourism, health care infrastructure developmentand health resource development; by applying commercial principlesand using ethical business practices while maintaining a reputation forquality.

Founder of forty nine 57

Dr Bright Eric Ohene

MD, PhD, is a Ghanaian cardiovascular surgeon, Invasive Cardiologistbiomedical scientist, expert in diseases of poverty, international medicineand public health advocate and motivational speaker.

Dr Ohene has a deep understanding of Asian business culture, and hasexpertise in high level negotiations and health diplomacy.

He has vast knowledge in Healthcare System Improvement having consultedin this field for many year.

where we want to be

Our main goal is to improve the service and industry standards inGhana when it comes to Health and Hospitality. We aim to achieve thisthrough a multilayered approach. We bring a vast business andprofessional network spanning across four continents.

We have a management team with 30+ years combined experience inthe Healthcare, Education and Hospitality sector. We work together todevelop services and solutions to meet the needs of our customers.

Our business network both locally and internationally are the corepillars of Forty Nine 57 and the growth we foresee. We strive to develop new and innovative products & services to meet the growingneeds of healthcare, Education and Hospitality in Ghana, Africa andfurther afield.

We also aim to push ourselves to improve the service and industrystandards in Ghana when it comes to health and Hospitality.
